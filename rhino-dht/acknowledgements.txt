Rhino DHT is making use of the builtin Esent database and the ManagedEsent project (http://codeplex.com/ManagedEsent),
Google's Protocol Buffers using dotnet-protobufs (http://github.com/jskeet/dotnet-protobufs/tree/master) from Jon Skeet
and of the Wintellect Power Threading (http://www.wintellect.com/PowerThreading.aspx) library
